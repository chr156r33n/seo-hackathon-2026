    <h2 class="h2--mono">About</h2>
    <p>
      The SEO Hackathon 2026 is a remote hackathon run synchronously over a 48-hour period.
      The goal is to build a solution to a real problem - a new workflow, capability, or tool that makes SEO work better.
    </p>
    <p>
      We're aiming for MVPs that deliver incremental impact, not sprawling "someday" platforms.
      If it's useful, shippable, and improves something in the real world, it counts.
    </p>
    <p>
      If you think you want to join check out the link below. Exact date still to be determined, but we're aiming for late feb.
    </p>

    <h2 class="h2--mono">How it works</h2>

    <ol class="steps steps--hacky">
      <li><strong>Teams:</strong> SEOs can form teams of up to 3 people. Solo teams are also welcome.</li>
      <li><strong>Comms:</strong> We'll run a closed WhatsApp group for participants during the event.</li>
      <li><strong>Build:</strong> Create an MVP that delivers an incremental improvement or new capability.</li>
      <li><strong>Commit:</strong> At the end, we'll commit everything into a shared repo.</li>
      <li><strong>Document:</strong> We'll generate <code>README.md</code> files for each project.</li>
      <li><strong>Show:</strong> We'll record lightning walkthroughs of each project and publish them (location TBC).</li>
    </ol>
